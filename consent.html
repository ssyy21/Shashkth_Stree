Understanding Consent and Boundaries in Relationships

Understanding and respecting consent and boundaries are essential for building trusting, healthy relationships. Consent is about giving permission and ensuring mutual agreement in all interactions, whether physical, emotional, or social. Boundaries, on the other hand, are personal limits that help protect your well-being and ensure that your needs are met with respect.
